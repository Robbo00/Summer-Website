Activities

The Beach

Sun, sand, and salty hair. Perfect spot to relax, tan, or pretend you’re in a travel commercial.

Water Park

Slides, splashes, and non-stop giggles. Just don’t race your friends… unless you’re ready to lose.

Swimming

Dive in, cool off, and feel like a mermaid—or a very fast sea turtle.

Amusement Park

Roller coasters, cotton candy, and way too many selfies. The perfect recipe for fun!

Hiking

Fresh air, epic views, and that satisfying feeling of earning your snack at the top.

Local Parks

Picnic blankets, frisbee throws, and squirrels judging your snack choices.
